Our specialised team has the skill and experience to provide a range of services. The utmost care is taken with all our work. The main streams of work we perform are as follows:

Property Law Practice

The first contact with law for many people is when buying a first home. We can assist you through this process, managing the details and offering advice. When or if the time comes, we'll also assist with selling your home.

We have expertise in the consent process for new developments and improvements, as well as helping you manage assets through trusts.

Dispute Resolution

If you're looking to resolve disputes, we offer solid advice and assistance with creating and reading agreements. We have a proven track record handling disputes.

Corporate, Secretariat and Commercial Practice

From structuring and mergers to shareholder agreements, we are here to assist you when starting, buying, running or selling your business.

Policy Formulation

At Holm Legal, we assist Organisations, Government Institutions and Management in formulating policies in functional areas of the Economy. We also advocate for implementation of reform policies in various sectors of the economy. We also conduct regular review of policies to reflect current international best practices, and improve efficiency in administration.

Intellectual Property

We offer world class services in the field of intellectual property from registration of Trademarks, Patent and Copyright to regular updates on Intellectual Property development both in Nigeria and worldwide. As a firm we do not stop after registration, we give regular updates about how to further protect and expand your Intellectual Property interest.

Infrastructure

In addition to our wide practice, we are skilled in taking on public private partnerships contracts of high value construction projects and drive the execution of effective airtight contracts with Government institutions and international organizations, so as to secure investments of our clients at all times.
