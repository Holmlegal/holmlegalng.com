PROTECTION AGAINST DISCRIMINATION FOR COVID-19 SURVIVORS

It is clear that due to the Global pandemic caused by the Novel Corona Virus (COVID-19) disease, the Economic World order has been disrupted. Major sectors are experiencing catastrophic losses, and businesses are plunging at all time lows. Governments of the world are exploring various options to revamp their economy Post-COVID-19, Businesses and industry players are going back to their drawing board to develop innovative, workable strategies to tackle the situation and provide an effortless transition for the operations of their business. Vitimization and Discrimination of COVID-19 Survivors, will be experienced most especially in the workplace.
